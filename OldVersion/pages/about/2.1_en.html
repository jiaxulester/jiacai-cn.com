Jiacai Hardware Co., Ltd. is a professional machining company that integrates various machining, metal processing, lathe parts processing, CNC machining center processing, screw nut processing. The factory now has Japan imported CNC numerical control lathe, automatic lathe, CNC machining center, production equipment and testing equipment, using high-quality raw materials and professional surface treatment, to produce first-class hardware products for our customers. And has passed ISO9001: 2008 quality system certification. The factory mainly produces and processes small household electrical shafts, digital products, automotive parts and hardware parts.

Main production equipment: more than 30 automatic lathes, 22 CNC cutting machines, 18 Japan imported Tsugami Nomura CNC core machines (203,205 core machine), 10 CNC machining centers, screw forming machine, screws Threading machine, rolling machine, grinding machine, milling machine, engraving machine, one type of drilling and tapping machine, automatic tapping machine, instrument lathe, milling machine, etc.

Equipments -

Main testing equipment: vernier caliper, watch card, spiral micrometer (micrometer), two dimensional filming, thread detection pass gauge, thread plug gauge, thread ring gauge, thermometer, salt spray tester, projector and so on. Auxiliary production equipment: grinding and polishing machine, precision cutting machine, etc.

Other Devices -

Main products: The company specializes in producing all kinds of screws, stamping, molds, nut nuts, gaskets, circlips, CNC precision computer lathes, precision automatic lathes and processing and forming hardware products with stamping and die casting.
Products involved in the field: precision electronic products (mobile phones, digital cameras, printers, computer cases, copiers, fax machines, circuit boards, etc.); electrical industry, toys (skating shoes, etc.), lighting (LED flashlights, lamp holders, etc.), furniture, Sanitary, sports equipment (golf, etc.), automotive and other industries.
